Rab di rasoi

Rab di rasoi is a bakery that specialises in Customized bakery products. It started a bakery situated in Amroha. Established in 2019 by a home baker. Rab di rasoi is now Amroha's #1 best rated Designer cake bakery.

We thrives to provide you unique and most personalized delicacies. We believe that we are not selling just cakes. Rather a cake is an expression by someone for someone to express their love and our clients entrust us with that responsibility to make that expression as beautiful as possible. We acknowledge our role very passionately.
